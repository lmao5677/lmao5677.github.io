Stick Ranger K.CO Mod Plan

 

The KCO Mod will introduce 4 new classes; the Assassin, who uses daggers to stab enemies and can cloak (This gets stronger if they have more MAG) uponing finishing an enemy, the Bomber, who throws bombs at enemies that explode and upon increasing its MAG the class can increase the amount of bombs thrown, the Soldier, who can wield machine guns that cost way more to fire than the regular Gunner, and the Lumberjack, who uses their axe to swing at enemies.

 

This mod will also contain new stages and will replace all the old ones. It will also contain new weapons for the 8 default classes.
